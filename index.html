About Inferencia

As an academic year neared its conclusion, a group of budding statisticians, students about to graduate from college, decided to pioneer a fest centred solely around statistics.

Inferencia has provided a remarkable opportunity to delve deeper into the vast realm of statistics. The phenomenal enthusiasm of all teachers and students in this fest has never failed to amaze us.

With illuminating seminars, engaging discussions, and interactive quizzes, we are excited to bring you the Fourth Edition of Inferencia. Be part of the only Statistical Fest in Town and experience an event like never before!
